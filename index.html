What is BMI?

  1. The body mass index (BMI), is a measure of relative weight based on an individual's mass and height.
  2. Normal (healthy weight) ranges from BMI 18.5 to 25.

Inputs

  • Height (in centimeters)
  • Weight (in kilo's)

Output

  • BMI
